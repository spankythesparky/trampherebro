Reporte Trampin de Hoy · July 23, 2026

Trampin the IBEW Indoor Locals — Thursday, July 23, 2026

LU-481 Indianapolis, IN is running hot with $58.70/hr top scale on the Google Data Center in Mooresville and Lilly facilities in Lebanon. Industrial Electric's second and third shift work at full scale is eating books fast—13-day rule means rotation, but the money's there. Ermco's outdoor new construction hits $58.70 with extensive background checks; Fluor pushing 10% overscale on Lilly work. This is JW meat-and-potatoes pay in the Midwest.

LU-291 Boise, ID has 27 calls but tight hands (111 on book). Prime Controls and VECA Electric both firing on Micron ID1 PLA at $45.33/hr with $170/day per diem — that's $1,190/week housing money if you're cleared. Background checks are the gate; reference numbers are live. Non-PLA Rosendin admin work bumps to $200/day travel pay.

LU-401 Reno, NV is 22 calls deep with $57.39/hr top scale. NEWTRON's Telecom/VDV fiber splicing gig includes $100/day per diem, $200/week structure—Sr. Tech rates with splicing cert required. BOMBARD, CUPERTINO, and BRIGGS all stacked at that rate; all OT is double time. No Monday onboarding on Project RNO is real friction.

LU-237 Niagara Falls, NY sits at 9 calls and 143 hands—best book-to-call ratio on the board. Lake Mariner Data Center is a triple-threat with Ferguson (69 calls), O'Connell (39), and BESCO (20) all running $48.20/hr, per diem available, first shift. Ferguson's Mon/Tue/Thu orientation beats O'Connell's Tuesday-only window.

LU-583 El Paso, TX has 10 calls chasing 107 hands. Summit Line and B&D Industries both hit $55/hr (NM JIW license required, EE98J); Rosendin's $40 base + $15 incentive for 90% of 60-hour weeks pushes closer to $55 with discipline. All OT double time. Fire alarm/VESDA exp gets premium placement.

Data centers are vacuuming inside hands nationwide—cooling, cable, controls, and PLC work are the real jobs; per diem and double-time OT are the recruiting tools.
