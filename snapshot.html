Where the work is right now — top-paying locals, the biggest boards, and the projects driving demand, pulled live from union dispatch.

Trampin the IBEW Indoor Locals — Thursday, July 23, 2026

LU-481 Indianapolis, IN is running hot with $58.70/hr top scale on the Google Data Center in Mooresville and Lilly facilities in Lebanon. Industrial Electric's second and third shift work at full scale is eating books fast—13-day rule means rotation, but the money's there. Ermco's outdoor new construction hits $58.70 with extensive background checks; Fluor pushing 10% overscale on Lilly work. This is JW meat-and-potatoes pay in the Midwest.

LU-291 Boise, ID has 27 calls but tight hands (111 on book). Prime Controls and VECA Electric both firing on Micron ID1 PLA at $45.33/hr with $170/day per diem — that's $1,190/week housing money if you're cleared. Background checks are the gate; reference numbers are live. Non-PLA Rosendin admin work bumps to $200/day travel pay.

LU-401 Reno, NV is 22 calls deep with $57.39/hr top scale. NEWTRON's Telecom/VDV fiber splicing gig includes $100/day per diem, $200/week structure—Sr. Tech rates with splicing cert required. BOMBARD, CUPERTINO, and BRIGGS all stacked at that rate; all OT is double time. No Monday onboarding on Project RNO is real friction.

LU-237 Niagara Falls, NY sits at 9 calls and 143 hands—best book-to-call ratio on the board. Lake Mariner Data Center is a triple-threat with Ferguson (69 calls), O'Connell (39), and BESCO (20) all running $48.20/hr, per diem available, first shift. Ferguson's Mon/Tue/Thu orientation beats O'Connell's Tuesday-only window.

LU-583 El Paso, TX has 10 calls chasing 107 hands. Summit Line and B&D Industries both hit $55/hr (NM JIW license required, EE98J); Rosendin's $40 base + $15 incentive for 90% of 60-hour weeks pushes closer to $55 with discipline. All OT double time. Fire alarm/VESDA exp gets premium placement.

Data centers are vacuuming inside hands nationwide—cooling, cable, controls, and PLC work are the real jobs; per diem and double-time OT are the recruiting tools.

Trampin the IBEW Lineman Locals — Thursday, July 23, 2026

LU-71 Columbus, OH is running HOT with 46 calls and ~140 hands — classic size imbalance. New River Electric Corp is the anchor, hiring 40 JW linemen on distribution work at top scale, 10-hour days with a $125 night stay bonus, plus another 9 slots in Southern Ohio on 4-10s. Capital Electric is calling for cable-splicing experience in Johnstown (OSHA 10 + Class A CDL required). This is bread-and-butter distro work, reliable and repeatable.

LU-583 El Paso, TX is screaming for inside JWs with $55/hr all-OT double time across four contractors. Summit Line leads with 45 bodies needed on a hot project; Rosendin is running a parallel call at $40 + $15 incentive for 90% utilization. B&D and Cupertino both need fire alarm and VESDA experience. New Mexico JIW license (EE98J) is the gate-keeper here — get it before you roll in.

LU-1426 Grand Forks, ND — $44.80/hr + $100/day non-taxed per diem, plus another $100/day if you carry ND license. Parsons Electric owns the board with 29 of the 21 calls split between the Polaris Forge Data Center (6-10s, $100 Mon-Fri incentive) and Hunt Electric's Air Base work (4-10s with 8 OT hours Fridays). Real ID required on base work. This is the cleanest per-diem stack on the board today.

LU-291 Boise, ID — 26 calls chasing Micron fab work. Inside JW scale sits at $45.33, but per diem hits $170/day x7 if eligible. Prime Controls, VECA, and Rosendin all staffing the same project; Rosendin bumps travel per diem to $200/day. PLA vs. non-PLA split across contractors — read the reference numbers closely before you hit dispatch.

LU-1547 Anchorage, AK has 44 calls on just 55 hands — thinnest ratio on the board. Golden Valley Electric's 8-week temp labor call pays $30.86/hr on week-on/week-off 10-12 hour rotations up in Healy. Alaska Communications has telecom JW slots at $45.31, and Wolf Creek is hiring fire alarm techs with NICET II ($55.84). Alaska work moves fast but demands patience.

LU-429 Nashville, TN — Newtron LLC's Google Clarksville megaproject is absorbing 66 of the 88 hands on the board. $120/day per diem if you're 50+ miles out, moving from 7-10s to 7-12s schedules mid-call. Three separate calls (day shift, night shift, secure building) give you flexibility on onboarding (M-F 7am). Day & Zimmermann has a smaller TVA play running 5-8s if you want steadier hours.

Data centers and mega-infrastructure are where the volume is: Nashville's Google build, Grand Forks' Polaris Forge, Boise's Micron fab, and El Paso's general surge. The per-diem game is winning right now — locals offering $100+ per day are eating the board.
