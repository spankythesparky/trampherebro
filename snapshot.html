Trampin the IBEW Indoor Locals — Tuesday, July 14, 2026

LU-481 Indianapolis, IN is running hot with $54.75/hr top scale and 43 calls chasing 188 hands. Meade Electric's got 23 JW slots on the Meta Data Center near Lebanon at $53.80/hr plus 10% over scale, running 5-12s Mon-Fri with Saturday OT. Ermco's Google play in Mooresville is 26 bodies, 5-10s plus 8-hour Saturdays. Book depth is real but the data center push keeps it moving.

LU-494 Milwaukee, WI is locked in on Port Washington's data center sprawl—three contractors (Gurtz, Cupertino, MJ Electric) are running 35-20 JW calls apiece, all paying JW wage plus $10/hr base increase, 50-60 hour weeks, drug test locked. Valid WI license required. Pieper Electric's got healthcare flex at 18 calls if the data center grind isn't your speed.

LU-1426 Grand Forks, ND has $58.50/hr top scale but Parsons Electric's Polaris Forge Data Center is paying $44.80/hr with $100-150/day non-taxed per diem (100+ mile radius). 45 combined calls across two Parsons tickets, 6-10s, strong incentive structure. Hunt Electric's Grand Forks Air Base offer is tighter at 10 calls but same tier pay.

LU-1105 Newark, OH runs 33 calls with no posted scale but THE SUPERIOR GROUP (30 JW) on Conesville is 4-10s and 1-8, most Saturdays, all OT paid as double. Lighthouse Electric's New Albany play is 22 calls at $5.75/hr over scale but mandates 45-hour minimums.

LU-26 Washington, DC has 40 calls, 185 hands available. JE Richards and FIS, LLC both staffing Sterling, VA data centers at M-F 8s with planned OT; FIS is posting $125/day per diem. Aarow Electric's Nolan Building (Springfield) is new construction, 3/4" to 4" EMT, military background preferred.

LU-134 Chicago, IL stays understaffed—only 165 hands against 40 calls. Elite Electric's Evanston gig is 30 JWs, 6 days/week 12 hours/day under Foreman Ron Spata.

The play is data center saturation: 970 open calls nationwide, but 60% are concentrated in five metros—every major contractor's stacked there, chasing the same 3,947 hands. Scale premium and per diem are becoming table stakes, not exceptions.

Trampin the IBEW Lineman Locals — Tuesday, July 14, 2026

LU-175 Chattanooga, TN has 33 inside JW calls, mostly Lawson Electric at the DNN4 Data Center in Dalton, GA—first shift, third shift (31.4% shift differential), and shop calls. Hire-ins are Wednesday mornings. Book shows 51 hands with quick turnover.

LU-291 Boise, ID features 24 inside JW calls on the Micron ID1 PLA job with VECA and Quality Electric, all $45.33/hr base with $170/day per diem (7 days if eligible) and a 4-10 schedule. St. Luke's Tower work pushes $55.33. Book depth 50 hands.

The market is chasing data centers and major utility grid work—transmission, distribution, and industrial backbone projects are driving the longest boards across the Midwest and Mountain West.
