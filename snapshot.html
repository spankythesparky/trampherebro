Trampin Day in Review

Where the work is right now — top-paying locals, the biggest boards, and the projects driving demand, pulled live from union dispatch.

Trampin the IBEW Indoor Locals — Monday, July 13, 2026

LU-481 Indianapolis, IN — 41 calls, 204 hands deep, and this board is printing money. Meade Electric's Meta Data Center gig pays $53.80/hr plus 10% over scale on July 15 and 20 — that's $59.18/hr for two days if you time it right. Google Data Center work through Ermco runs all new construction outside, heavy background requirements. Industrial Electric's IU Hospital call is indoor AC, 5-10s with Saturday shifts, OSHA 1 required. Top scale hits $54.75.

LU-1426 Grand Forks, ND — Parsons Electric is running dual crews at the Polaris Forge Data Center in Harwood with $44.80/hr base, but $100/day non-taxed per diem (100+ mile radius GPS) plus $100 incentive Mon-Fri, $150 weekends — that's north of $60/hr effective rate. Fifty JWs needed on one call, 28 on another. Hunt Electric's Grand Forks Air Base call runs 4-10s with Friday OT, same bonus structure. Board's got 130 hands and work won't stop.

LU-430 Racine, WI — Microsoft Mount Pleasant is the play: $62.51/hr across Gerdes, Pieper, Staff, and Kelso Burnett. Wisconsin Inside JW or reciprocal required; long sleeves and safety toes mandatory. Seventy-six hands on the board chasing 11 calls means Book 1 is nearly empty. Started late June, still ramping.

LU-237 Niagara Falls, NY — Lake Mariner Data Center dominates with O'Connell and Ferguson both working $48.20/hr, 7/10s first shift, orientations Monday only, per diem available. Amazon calls through BCU run same pay, varied schedules (5/10s + 8 Saturday or 7/10s). One hundred three hands competing for nine calls.

LU-613 Atlanta, GA — Inglett & Stubbs' Wellstar Paulding and QTS Fayette work pays $40/hr with optional Saturdays. Miller Electrical's Cable Splicer at QTS Fayette hits $40.90/hr but demands three years documented splicing — interview required. Thirty-eight calls on the board.

Market snapshot: Data centers are still the gravity well — Midwest and Mid-Atlantic data construction is consolidating hands faster than any other sector, with four-figure per diems and overtime premia pushing effective rates past $65/hr in cold climates where retention matters.

Trampin the IBEW Lineman Locals — Saturday, July 11, 2026

LU-71 Columbus, OH is sitting fat with 48 calls and $61.44/hr top scale. NEW RIVER ELECT. CORP. is running dual distro jobs—41 hands needed in Columbus proper with $125 per diem, nights stayed, plus a $100 bonus if you work every available day; another 10 hands scattered across Southern Ohio at the same rate. JJM POWER is pushing Chillicothe distro rebuilds at $100/day per diem on 4-man crews. The Thayer make-ready and reconduct work pays $500 for 45 hours, $600 for 55 hours with hotels covered. Book depth at roughly 133 hands suggests solid movement.

LU-1426 Grand Forks, ND has 26 calls but pay's the catch: Parsons Electric's Polaris Forge Data Center gig in Harwood is $44.80/hr for 6-10s, though Apex Line's Journeyman Lineman work hits $58.50/hr on 6-12s with $100/day incentive. Hunt Electric's Grand Forks Air Base work is 4-10s plus 8-hour OT Fridays at the same rate.

LU-1547 Anchorage, AK shows 46 calls against just 60 hands on book—tightest ratio here—but the scale tops $91,321.60 annually. Work's scattered: Golden Valley's Healy Powerhouse gig is inventory/moving at $30.86/hr for an 8-week call; Morley Electric has Inside JW work in Delta Junction with housing or per diem covered.

LU-291 Boise, ID is chasing Inside work at $65.33/hr with VECA Electric on META's KND sites (effective MOU 4/13/26), plus Lea Electric's Micron PLA job at $45.33/hr with $170/day per diem and $20/hr travel pay if you bite the Idaho run.

LU-583 El Paso, TX has 10 calls for Inside Journeymen requiring NM State JIW License EE98J. Summit, Cupertino, and B&D are all posting $55.00/hr on projects; Rosendin undercuts at $40/hr plus $15 incentive for 90% of a 60-hour week.

Data-center work and transmission buildout are eating calls coast-to-coast; the hands are chasing scale, per diem, and consistent schedules over distance.
